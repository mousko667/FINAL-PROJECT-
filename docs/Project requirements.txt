Digital and Secure Supplier Invoice Validation Management System
System Overview
Large organizations often face inefficiencies in supplier invoice handling due to paper-based processes, delayed validation cycles, limited traceability, and potential security vulnerabilities. Manual workflows can result in payment delays, duplication, and lack of transparency in financial operations.
This project proposes the design and development of a Digital and Secure Supplier Invoice Validation Management System. The system will automate the reception, processing, validation, approval, payment tracking, and digital archiving of supplier invoices.
The solution will integrate secure access controls, audit trails, and workflow automation to improve traceability, enhance data security, and optimize business process efficiency within the organization.
________________________________________
Objectives
General Objective
To design and implement a secure digital system that automates supplier invoice validation and enhances transparency, efficiency, and traceability in financial processes.
Specific Objectives
1.	To develop a digital invoice submission and reception module
2.	To implement automated validation and approval workflows
3.	To ensure secure storage and archiving of supplier invoices
4.	To provide real-time tracking of invoice status and payment progress
5.	To generate financial and audit reports supporting compliance and transparency
